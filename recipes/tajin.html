Tajin

Description

Tajin is a Moroccan dish, and it refers to a type of earthenware pot that is commonly used in Moroccan cuisine for cooking and serving food. The name "Tajin" also refers to the dish that is cooked and served in this pot, which typically includes meat (such as lamb or chicken), vegetables, and a variety of spices, including cumin, paprika, and saffron. The dish is often cooked over low heat for several hours to allow the flavors to meld together and create a rich, fragrant stew. Once the Tajin is cooked, it is often served with couscous or bread

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 kilo of potato
  • 1/2 kilo of onions
  • meat

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine all the ingredients until well mixed.
  2. Store the Tajin seasoning in an airtight container at room temperature, away from light and moisture.
