Rice with Fish

Description

Rice with fish is a popular dish in many cultures and can be prepared in a variety of ways depending on the region and culinary tradition. Here is a simple recipe for a delicious and easy-to-make rice and fish dish:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound white fish fillets (such as cod or tilapia)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Vegetables
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Broth

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice in a fine-mesh sieve and set aside to drain.
  2. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and red bell pepper and cook for another 2-3 minutes./li>
  4. Add the rice to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables.
  5. Fluff the couscous with a fork to separate the grains.
  6. Add the chicken broth, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper and stir to combine.
